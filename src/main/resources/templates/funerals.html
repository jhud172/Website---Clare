Supportive process

A calm step-by-step structure for families who need clarity and compassion.

01

Initial conversation

Clare speaks with the family or funeral director to understand the person, the service and the tone required.

02

Gathering memories

Stories, important details and meaningful moments are gently gathered to shape the tribute.

03

Writing the ceremony

The ceremony is written with dignity, sensitivity and a clear sense of the life being honoured.

04

Delivering the service

On the day, Clare provides a calm, respectful presence and clear pacing throughout the ceremony.

05

Follow-up support

Where appropriate, Clare can help ensure any final ceremony materials are shared clearly afterwards.

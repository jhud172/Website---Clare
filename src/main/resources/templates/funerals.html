Funerals and memorials

Compassionate ceremonies written with warmth, clarity and real care for the people in the room.

Clare's approach to funerals and memorials is steady, thoughtful and personal, helping families create something that feels respectful, honest and beautifully held.

Funeral and memorial ceremonies often need both structure and sensitivity. Clare works closely with families and, where needed, funeral directors to shape a service that reflects the person being remembered with warmth, dignity and care.

The focus is on listening well, gathering the right details, and creating a ceremony that feels personal without becoming overwhelming. The tone can be traditional, modern, reflective or gently celebratory depending on the family and the life being honoured.

Services can also be shaped for memorials, interments and unique locations where a more bespoke ceremony is needed.

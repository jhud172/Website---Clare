Why celebrant-led weddings

Flexible, personal and meaningful from beginning to end.

Celebrant-led weddings give couples more freedom to create a ceremony that feels truly personal, whether that means a different tone, more storytelling, family involvement or symbolic details that matter to them.

Clare works with couples to create something stylish, warm and genuinely reflective of their relationship. Where needed, legal marriage registration can be completed separately from the celebrant ceremony itself.

Symbolism and ritual

Handfasting, unity rituals and personal readings can all become part of the ceremony naturally.

Clare can incorporate handfasting, unity rituals, sand ceremonies, personal readings and other symbolic moments in a way that feels thoughtful rather than staged.

The aim is always to create depth and meaning without interrupting the emotional flow of the ceremony itself.

Wedding ceremonies

Modern ceremonies for couples who want something personal, polished and full of feeling.

Clare creates wedding ceremonies that feel like a true reflection of the couple at the centre of them, with the right balance of warmth, style and calm guidance.

Every wedding ceremony should sound like the people it belongs to. Clare works with couples to shape a ceremony that feels relaxed, beautifully paced and genuinely theirs rather than over-scripted or overly formal.

From the first conversation through to delivery on the day, the process is designed to feel clear and supportive. There is space for story, vows, readings, family involvement and those small details that make the whole thing feel personal.

This is a celebrant-led ceremony service. Any legal marriage requirements should always be confirmed separately where a registrar or other legal process is still required.

Helpful notes

Questions couples often need answered early.

Can the ceremony be fully bespoke?

Yes. The wording, tone and structure are designed around the couple rather than taken from a standard script.

Can family and friends be involved?

Absolutely. Readings, contributions and meaningful personal touches can all be built into the ceremony.

Is the ceremony legally binding?

Any legal marriage requirements should be confirmed separately. Clare's website does not make claims about legal marriage authority unless that position is formally confirmed.

Information collected

When you contact Clare Life Ceremonies, the website may collect your name, email address, phone number, ceremony type, preferred date, venue or location, and any details you include in your message.

How information is used

Your information is used to respond to your enquiry, discuss ceremony requirements, provide relevant information about services, and manage any next steps connected with your enquiry or booking.

How information is stored

Enquiry details may be stored in Clare’s email system and related business records where needed to respond properly, prepare services or keep appropriate administrative records.

Sharing information

Your data is not sold. Information is only shared where necessary to run the website, deliver communications, meet legal obligations or support a service you have asked Clare to provide.

Cookies and website data

This website may use essential website technologies needed for normal site operation. If broader analytics, cookie tools or embedded third-party services are added later, this section should be updated to reflect them accurately.

Your rights

You can ask for access to the personal information held about you, request corrections, ask for deletion where appropriate, or raise concerns about how your information is used.

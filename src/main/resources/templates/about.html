Clare's story

A new celebrant business built around people, care and meaningful moments.

Clare is moving into celebrancy with a clear sense of what people need when a day matters: someone who listens properly, communicates warmly and creates a ceremony that feels personal rather than formulaic.

Her work is rooted in helping people mark important moments with the right balance of emotion, calm structure and thoughtful detail. Whether the occasion is joyful or reflective, Clare wants the people involved to feel seen, supported and genuinely looked after.

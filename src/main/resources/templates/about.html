About Clare

A celebrant style built around warmth, steadiness and personal detail.

As Clare moves from a long career in policing into celebrancy, she is bringing the same composure, care and sense of responsibility into a more personal kind of work.

Clare Brunton Life Ceremonies has been shaped around the idea that important days deserve more than a standard script. They deserve words, pacing and presence that feel thoughtful from the first hello to the final goodbye.

After years of working in a professional public-facing role, Clare is now building a celebrant practice that feels warm, modern and deeply human. Her approach is calm and organised, but never cold. The aim is always to create a ceremony that feels personal, beautifully put together and true to the people at the centre of it.

Whether the day is joyful, reflective or somewhere in between, Clare wants clients to feel held, listened to and confident that the ceremony will sound like them, not like something borrowed from a template.
